Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Breathe provide?

Breathe cover all major home maintenance tasks including: Gutter & roof cleaning, HVAC system maintenance, appliance maintenance, winterization, seasonal yard work, safety testing (radon, lead, etc), pest prevention, and more.

How does the pricing work?

Breathe is an annual subscription for $400 per month.

I just purchased a home. Can I use Breathe?

Absolutely! In fact, we can help you track all of the work done on your home over its lifetime. If you ever have a question about the house we can also connect you directly to the real estate agents who helped with the sale. Finally, if you decide to sell, all your home's upkeep information is in one easy place to showcase to the new buyer.

How do I sign up for Breathe?

Signing up is easy! First, check if Breathe is available in your zip code using the tool above. If we are, you can then proceed to fill out your details to get started with a worry-free home maintenance experience.
